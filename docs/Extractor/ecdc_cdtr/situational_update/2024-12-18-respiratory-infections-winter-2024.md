# Acute respiratory infections in the EU/EEA: epidemiological update and current public health recommendations – winter 2024/2025

## 基本資訊
- **來源**: ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report
- **發布日期**: 2024-12-18
- **疾病**: Acute respiratory infections (influenza and RSV)
- **地理位置**: EU/EEA countries

## 事件摘要
Almost all countries reporting data to ECDC are observing sharp increases in indicators of both influenza and respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) activity, with a visible impact in secondary care.

## 流行病學資訊
- **病例數**: Sharp increases (specific numbers not provided in RSS)
- **傳播模式**: Seasonal increase (winter 2024/2025)
- **受影響地區**: Almost all EU/EEA countries reporting data
- **病原體**: Influenza and respiratory syncytial virus (RSV)
- **醫療系統影響**: Visible impact in secondary care

## 公衛意義
- Widespread increases across almost all reporting countries indicate significant seasonal surge
- Impact on secondary care suggests strain on healthcare systems
- Dual burden of influenza and RSV creates compounded pressure on health services
- Winter timing consistent with expected seasonal respiratory infection patterns
- Public health recommendations included in full report
