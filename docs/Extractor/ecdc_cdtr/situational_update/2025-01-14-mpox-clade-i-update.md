# Epidemiological update, 14 January 2025: Mpox due to monkeypox virus clade I

## 基本資訊
- **來源**: ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report
- **發布日期**: 2025-01-14
- **疾病**: Mpox (monkeypox)
- **病原體變異株**: Monkeypox virus clade I
- **地理位置**: EU/EEA countries

## 事件摘要
In recent months there have been reports of monkeypox virus clade I cases in different EU/EEA countries following the geographic expansion of monkeypox virus clade I since mid-2024. This epidemiological update summarises these recent events.

## 流行病學資訊
- **病例數**: Multiple cases across different EU/EEA countries (specific numbers not provided in RSS)
- **傳播模式**: Geographic expansion from mid-2024 onwards
- **受影響地區**: Multiple EU/EEA countries
- **病毒株**: Clade I (historically associated with more severe disease compared to clade II)

## 公衛意義
- Geographic expansion of clade I into EU/EEA represents a significant epidemiological development
- Clade I historically associated with higher case fatality rates than clade II
- Multiple countries affected indicates potential for continued spread within the region
- Timeline (mid-2024 onwards) shows sustained transmission over several months
