# Two cases of Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS) in travellers returning to France from the Arabian Peninsula

## 基本資訊
- **來源**: ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report
- **發布日期**: 2025-12-05
- **疾病**: Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS)
- **地理位置**: France (imported from Arabian Peninsula)

## 事件摘要
On 3 December 2025, the French Ministry of Health reported two imported human Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS) cases with travel history to the Arabian Peninsula.

## 流行病學資訊
- **病例數**: 2 imported cases

- **受影響地區**: France (origin: Arabian Peninsula)

## 公衛意義
- Two imported MERS cases indicate ongoing transmission risk in the Arabian Peninsula
- Travel-associated cases highlight importance of surveillance for returning travelers
- MERS-CoV continues to pose a public health concern for travelers to endemic regions
