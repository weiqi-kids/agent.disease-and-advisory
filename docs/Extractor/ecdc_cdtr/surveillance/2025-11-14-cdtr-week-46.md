# Communicable disease threats report, 8 - 14 November 2025, week 46

## 基本資訊
- **發布日期**: 2025-11-14
- **報告週期**: Week 46 (8-14 November 2025)
- **來源**: ECDC CDTR
- **分類**: surveillance

## 內容摘要
This issue of the ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report (CDTR) covers the period 8-14 November 2025 and includes updates on measles, dengue, mpox, respiratory viruses, West Nile virus, dengue, chikungunya, Ebola and poliovirus detection in wastewater.

## 涵蓋疾病
- Measles
- Dengue
- Mpox
- Respiratory viruses
- West Nile virus
- Chikungunya
- Ebola
- Poliovirus (wastewater detection)

## 備註
定期監測報告，涵蓋多種傳染病威脅的最新流行病學更新。本期包含廢水中脊髓灰質炎病毒檢測的更新。
