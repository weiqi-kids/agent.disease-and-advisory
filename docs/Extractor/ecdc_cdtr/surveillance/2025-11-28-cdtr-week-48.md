# Communicable disease threats report, 22 - 28 November 2025, week 48

## 基本資訊
- **發布日期**: 2025-11-28
- **報告週期**: Week 48 (22 - 28 November 2025)
- **來源**: ECDC CDTR
- **分類**: surveillance

## 內容摘要
This issue of the ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report (CDTR) covers the period 22 - 28 November 2025 and includes updates on respiratory virus epidemiology in the EU/EEA, chikungunya, West Nile virus, Marburg virus disease, influenza A, cholera, swine influenza, hepatitis A, and Ebola.

## 涵蓋疾病
- Respiratory viruses (EU/EEA epidemiology)
- Chikungunya
- West Nile virus
- Marburg virus disease
- Influenza A
- Cholera
- Swine influenza
- Hepatitis A
- Ebola

## 備註
定期監測報告，涵蓋多種傳染病威脅的最新流行病學更新。
