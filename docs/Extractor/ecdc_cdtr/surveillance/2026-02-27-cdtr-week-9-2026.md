## 詳細內容

This issue of the ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report (CDTR) covers the period 21 - 27 February 2026 and includes updates on respiratory virus epidemiology in the EU/EEA, mass gathering monitoring, chikungunya, cholera, and swine flu.

Key topics covered in this weekly surveillance report:

- **Respiratory virus epidemiology**: Monitoring of influenza, SARS-CoV-2, and RSV circulation across EU/EEA member states.
- **Mass gathering monitoring**: Public health surveillance related to mass gatherings.
- **Chikungunya**: Updates on chikungunya cases and transmission relevant to Europe.
- **Cholera**: Monitoring of cholera activity with potential implications for the EU/EEA.
- **Swine flu**: Updates on swine-origin influenza virus activity.
