## 詳細內容

This issue of the ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report (CDTR) covers the period 28 February–6 March 2026 and includes updates on respiratory virus epidemiology in the EU/EEA, SARS-CoV-2 variant classification, MERS, mass gathering monitoring, and chikungunya.

Key topics covered in this weekly surveillance report:

- **Respiratory virus epidemiology**: Ongoing monitoring of influenza, SARS-CoV-2, and RSV circulation patterns across EU/EEA member states.
- **SARS-CoV-2 variant classification**: Updates on circulating variants and their classification status.
- **MERS**: Monitoring of Middle East Respiratory Syndrome coronavirus activity.
- **Mass gathering monitoring**: Surveillance related to public health risks at mass gatherings.
- **Chikungunya**: Updates on chikungunya activity relevant to the EU/EEA region.
