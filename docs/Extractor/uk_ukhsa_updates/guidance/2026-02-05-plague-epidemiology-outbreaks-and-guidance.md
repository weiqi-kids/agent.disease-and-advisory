## 摘要

Comprehensive guidance document covering the epidemiology, symptoms, diagnosis and management of plague, a serious bacterial infection caused by Yersinia pestis.

## 詳細內容

This guidance provides detailed information on plague, covering:

- **Epidemiology**: Distribution and transmission patterns of plague globally
- **Symptoms**: Clinical presentation including bubonic, septicemic, and pneumonic forms
- **Diagnosis**: Laboratory testing and diagnostic procedures
- **Management**: Treatment protocols and infection control measures

Plague is a bacterial infection caused by Yersinia pestis, primarily transmitted through flea bites or contact with infected animals. While rare in the UK, this guidance is essential for healthcare providers who may encounter imported cases or need to provide advice to travelers visiting endemic areas.
