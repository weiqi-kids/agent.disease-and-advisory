## 摘要

英國健康安全署 Brucella Reference Unit (BRU) 提供布魯氏菌屬物種的實驗室診斷和臨床管理服務，為醫療機構和公衛單位提供專業檢驗與諮詢支援。

## 詳細內容

The Brucella Reference Unit (BRU) provides laboratory diagnostic and clinical management services for Brucella species. This is a reference service offering specialized testing and clinical guidance for healthcare providers dealing with brucellosis cases.
