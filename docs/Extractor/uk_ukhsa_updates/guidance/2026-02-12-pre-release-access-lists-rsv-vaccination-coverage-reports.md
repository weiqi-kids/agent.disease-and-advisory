## 摘要

英國健康安全署公布呼吸道合胞病毒（RSV）孕婦疫苗接種覆蓋率報告及老年人疫苗接種覆蓋率報告的預先發布訪問名單，列出有權提前獲取報告的單位與人員。

## 詳細內容

Lists of those receiving pre-release access to the UK Health Security Agency respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) maternal vaccination coverage reports, and the respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) older adults vaccination coverage reports. This ensures transparency in data dissemination and identifies stakeholders with early access to vaccination coverage statistics.
