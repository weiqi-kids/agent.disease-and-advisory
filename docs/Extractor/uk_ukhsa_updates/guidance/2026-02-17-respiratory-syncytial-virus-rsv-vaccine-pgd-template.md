Patient Group Direction (PGD) template to support the national vaccination programme for pregnant women to protect infants and older adults aged 75 years and above and residents in care homes for older adults.

此指引文件為醫療從業人員提供標準化的疫苗接種指示範本，確保 RSV 疫苗接種計畫能夠安全且有效地在全國範圍內實施。
