## 摘要

UKHSA guidance on how to avoid becoming unwell while visiting a farm. Farm environments may expose visitors to various zoonotic pathogens, and this guidance aims to reduce infection risk, particularly for children.

## 詳細內容

How to avoid becoming unwell while visiting a farm. Key prevention advice includes thorough handwashing after contact with animals, avoiding eating or drinking in animal areas, and paying special attention to children's hygiene practices. Common farm-visit associated infections include E. coli O157, Cryptosporidiosis, and Salmonellosis.

## 相關疾病

- E. coli
- Cryptosporidiosis
- Salmonellosis
