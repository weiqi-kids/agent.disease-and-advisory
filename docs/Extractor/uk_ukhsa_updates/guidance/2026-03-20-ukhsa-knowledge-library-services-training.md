## 摘要

UKHSA 知識與圖書館服務為合格工作人員提供各種資訊技能培訓，包含團體及一對一培訓課程。

## 詳細內容

Knowledge and Library Services provide a range of information skills training for eligible staff. Training is available in group or one-to-one settings.
