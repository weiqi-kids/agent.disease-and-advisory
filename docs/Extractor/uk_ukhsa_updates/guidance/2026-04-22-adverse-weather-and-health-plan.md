## 摘要

不良天氣與健康計劃（AWHP）整合了英格蘭原有的熱浪計劃與寒冷天氣計劃，旨在保護個人和社區免受極端天氣對健康的影響，並提升社區韌性。計劃涵蓋熱浪、嚴寒、洪水及乾旱等多種極端天氣類型，由公共部門、NHS England、獨立及志願組織、健康與社會照護提供者共同協調實施。計劃並與氣象局合作的天氣健康預警系統整合，提供主動式公衛回應機制。

## 詳細內容

The Adverse Weather and Health Plan (AWHP) is a UK government initiative that consolidates previous separate response frameworks, replacing the earlier standalone Heatwave Plan for England and Cold Weather Plan for England. It aims to protect individuals and communities from the health effects of adverse weather and to build community resilience. The plan addresses multiple weather threats, including heat, cold, flooding, and drought. Implementation involves coordinated effort across the public sector, NHS England, independent and voluntary organizations, health and social care providers, and local communities to develop integrated planning and response mechanisms. The plan is supported by scientific evidence, guidance materials, and the Weather-Health Alerts system in partnership with the Met Office.
