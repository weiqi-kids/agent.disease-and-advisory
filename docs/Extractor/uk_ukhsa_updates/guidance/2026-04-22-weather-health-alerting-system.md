## 摘要

UKHSA 與氣象局（Met Office）合作管理的天氣健康預警系統，在極端氣溫可能影響民眾健康時向醫療與社會照護部門提供早期預警。系統分為熱健康警報（6 月 1 日至 9 月 30 日）與冷健康警報（11 月 1 日至 3 月 31 日）兩部分，並可在特殊情況下於這些期間之外發出警報。預警信息透過電子郵件發送給登記受眾，目標對象包括應急回應者、志願組織及政府部門。

## 詳細內容

The Weather-Health Alerting System, managed by UKHSA in partnership with the Met Office, provides early warning to the health and social care sector when adverse temperatures threaten public health. The system comprises two components: Heat-Health Alerts (HHA) operating June 1–September 30, and Cold-Health Alerts (CHA) running November 1–March 31, with extraordinary alerts issued outside these periods when criteria are met. The system reaches registered recipients via email and targets multiple stakeholders including responders, voluntary organizations, and government departments to coordinate protective actions during temperature extremes. A user guide explains what each alert colour means in terms of expected health impacts.
