## 摘要

UKHSA 更新了針對注射藥物使用者的破傷風防治指引，提供臨床人員分發給患者使用。該指引說明破傷風由產生神經毒素的梭狀芽孢桿菌引起，孢子可能存在於動物糞便中，注射藥物使用者為高風險族群。此資源旨在提升此脆弱族群的風險意識，並鼓勵其尋求適當醫療支援以降低感染風險。

## 詳細內容

This UK Health Security Agency resource is designed for clinicians to distribute to patients who inject drugs. The guidance explains that tetanus results from a neurotoxin produced by *Clostridium tetani*, an anaerobic spore-forming bacillus with spores potentially present in animal waste. The material aims to raise awareness among people who inject drugs about tetanus infection risks and encourage them to seek appropriate medical support for risk reduction. Published in 2013 and updated in April 2026, this clinical resource addresses a serious health concern for this vulnerable population.
