## 摘要

UKHSA 發布支援抗菌素耐藥性意識週（WAAW）及歐洲抗生素意識日（EAAD）的醫療衛生專業人員工具包，鼓勵醫療從業者承諾負責任地使用抗菌素，推動「抗生素守護者」運動。

## 詳細內容

The UK Health Security Agency published an updated toolkit (June 2026) to support planning activities for World Antimicrobial Resistance Awareness Week (WAAW) and European Antibiotic Awareness Day (EAAD).

**Target Audience:**
- Healthcare providers in England
- Local authorities
- Universities and professional organisations
- Health students and pre-registration professionals

**Key Purpose:**
The toolkit promotes responsible antimicrobial stewardship through the "Antibiotic Guardian" campaign initiative. Healthcare professionals are encouraged to commit to responsible use of antimicrobials and to lead awareness activities within their organisations and communities.

**Geographic Scope:**
This guidance applies specifically to England; the underlying WAAW and EAAD campaigns operate internationally.

**Background:**
Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is one of the greatest global public health threats. When antibiotics and other antimicrobials lose effectiveness, infections become harder to treat, increasing the risk of disease spread, severe illness, and death. Regular campaigns such as WAAW and EAAD aim to preserve the effectiveness of existing antimicrobials through education and behaviour change.
