## 摘要

UKHSA 與 NHS England 聯合發布腦膜炎球菌 B 型（MenB）限時疫苗接種計畫，提供符合資格人群 MenB 保護，並向醫療專業人員提供接種建議、資格標準及配套資源。

## 詳細內容

The UK Health Security Agency and NHS England published a time-limited Meningococcal B (MenB) vaccination offer in June 2026, targeting specific eligible populations.

**What is MenB?**
Meningococcal B (MenB) is a bacterial infection caused by Neisseria meningitidis group B, which can lead to life-threatening bacterial meningitis and septicaemia (blood poisoning). MenB accounts for the majority of meningococcal disease cases in the UK.

**Nature of This Offer:**
This is a time-limited initiative — a temporary vaccination programme beyond the standard national immunisation schedule. The publication includes:
- Background rationale for establishing the temporary offer
- Eligibility criteria determining who qualifies for the vaccination
- Recommendations for administration of MenB vaccination (aimed at healthcare professionals)
- Supporting resources (Annex A) for implementation

**Target Audience:**
Healthcare professionals responsible for identifying eligible individuals and administering or recommending the vaccine.

**Note:** Specific eligibility criteria and detailed clinical guidance are contained within the full letter and accompanying Annex A documents available through the GOV.UK publications portal.

## 相關疾病

Meningococcal B disease, bacterial meningitis, septicaemia
