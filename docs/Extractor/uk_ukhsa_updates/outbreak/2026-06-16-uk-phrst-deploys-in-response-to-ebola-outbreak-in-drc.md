## 摘要

UKHSA 宣布部署 7 名 UK 公共衛生快速支援團隊（UK-PHRST）專業人員，協助 WHO 回應剛果民主共和國（DRC）及周邊地區的伊波拉疫情。英國承諾提供 2,100 萬英鎊支持 DRC 政府主導的應對工作。UKHSA 評估英國境內公眾風險極低。

## 詳細內容

The UK Health Security Agency is responding to an Ebola outbreak in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) and the broader region by deploying specialists from the UK Public Health Rapid Support Team (UK-PHRST).

**Deployment Details (7 specialists total):**
- **4 specialists** stationed in Eastern DRC supporting WHO field operations on infection prevention and control, epidemiology, and community engagement
- **1 Risk Communication and Community Engagement (RCCE) specialist** based at the WHO country office in Kinshasa
- **1 epidemiologist** deployed to Republic of Congo for regional support
- **1 data modelling expert** providing remote assistance

**UK Risk Assessment:**
"The risk to the public in the UK remains very low."

**Financial Commitment:**
The UK government committed **£21 million** to support the DRC government-led response.

**Academic Partnership:**
An **£18 million five-year academic partnership** renewal was announced between UKHSA and the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine to strengthen epidemic preparedness research across Africa and Southeast Asia.

**Background on UK-PHRST:**
- Established in 2016 following the West Africa Ebola crisis
- Has conducted over 50 deployments involving 100+ personnel over nine years
- Includes 10 prior deployments during the 2018-2019 North Kivu Ebola outbreak

Ebola virus disease (EVD)

## 影響地區

Democratic Republic of Congo (Eastern DRC, Kinshasa), Republic of Congo, Central Africa
