## 詳細內容

Letter from Health Minister Ashley Dalton MP confirming the UK Health Security Agency's role and priorities for the financial year 2025 to 2026. This strategic document sets the direction for UKHSA's work across all areas of health security and protection.

**UKHSA's core mission:**
- Protect the public from infectious diseases and environmental hazards
- Provide national leadership in health security
- Support NHS and local authorities with expert advice
- Conduct surveillance and research
- Respond to health emergencies

**Typical priority areas likely include:**
- **Pandemic preparedness**: Strengthening readiness for future health emergencies
- **Routine immunization**: Maintaining and improving vaccination coverage
- **Antimicrobial resistance**: Tackling the growing AMR threat
- **Health inequalities**: Addressing disparities in health protection
- **Climate and health**: Responding to environmental health threats
- **Laboratory capacity**: Maintaining diagnostic and research capabilities
- **International collaboration**: Working with global health partners
- **Workforce development**: Building expert capacity in health protection

**Strategic significance:**
This annual priorities document:
- Provides accountability and transparency
- Aligns UKHSA activities with government health policy
- Sets performance expectations
- Guides resource allocation
- Informs stakeholder planning

**Stakeholder relevance:**
The priorities inform:
- NHS planning and commissioning
- Local authority public health strategies
- Academic and research partnerships
- International health cooperation
- Industry engagement (vaccines, diagnostics, therapeutics)

Understanding UKHSA's priorities helps stakeholders align their efforts with national health security objectives and anticipate areas of focus for the year ahead.
