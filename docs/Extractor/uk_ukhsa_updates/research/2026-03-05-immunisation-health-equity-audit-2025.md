## 摘要

UKHSA published the 2025 Health Equity Audit of the National Immunisation programme, aligned with the UKHSA Immunisation Equity Strategy 2025 to 2030. The audit assesses vaccination coverage inequalities across different population groups to ensure equitable access to immunisation services.

## 詳細內容

The 2025 audit of the National Immunisation programme aligned with the UKHSA Immunisation Equity Strategy 2025 to 2030. This systematic assessment examines vaccination coverage disparities across different demographic groups, including ethnicity, socioeconomic status, and geographic region. The audit aims to identify gaps in vaccine uptake and inform strategies to reduce health inequalities in immunisation coverage.
