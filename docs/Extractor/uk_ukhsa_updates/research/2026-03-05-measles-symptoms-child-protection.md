## 詳細內容

Measles is highly contagious, so even a small decline in vaccine uptake can lead to a rise in cases. There has been a resurgence of measles in England, and around the world, in recent years. In 2024 there were 2,911 laboratory confirmed measles cases in England, the highest number of cases recorded annually in decades.

Key points:
- Measles has an extremely high basic reproduction number (R0 ~12-18)
- The MMR vaccine is the most effective prevention measure
- Children should receive two doses of MMR vaccine on schedule
- If unvaccinated or incompletely vaccinated, catch-up vaccination is recommended
- Parents should seek medical attention if measles symptoms appear
