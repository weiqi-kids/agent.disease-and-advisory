## 摘要

UKHSA published its 2023 to 2033 science strategy, outlining the agency's scientific assets, capabilities and ambitions. The strategy aims to protect the nation's health from current and future health threats while securing prosperity through science-driven public health approaches.

## 詳細內容

This corporate report outlines the scientific assets, capabilities and ambitions of UKHSA, and the plan to protect the nation's health from current and future health threats. The strategy covers a ten-year period from 2023 to 2033, providing long-term direction for UKHSA's scientific capabilities in areas including infectious disease surveillance, outbreak response, laboratory science, and public health research.
