## 摘要

UKHSA 發布漢他病毒人傳人感染傳播參數系統性文獻回顧（納入 17 項研究，證據截至 2026 年 5 月）。關鍵發現：人傳人傳播僅見於安地斯病毒（Andes virus），且目前尚無直接證據能確定感染者的傳染期長度。

## 詳細內容

The UK Health Security Agency published a systematic evidence review on hantavirus human-to-human transmission parameters, initially published 19 May 2026 with updates through 12 June 2026.

**Study Overview:**
- Systematic review of evidence through 1 May 2026
- **17 studies** included in the final analysis
- 64-page systematic evidence summary (PDF format)

**Key Finding:**
**Human-to-human transmission of hantavirus is only reported for Andes virus (ANDV).** No other hantavirus species demonstrates sustained human-to-human transmission.

**Transmission Parameters Examined:**

| Parameter | Key Finding |
|-----------|-------------|
| Incubation period | Ranges reported; revised in June 2026 update based on updated study classifications |
| Infectious period | **No direct evidence identified** — significant knowledge gap |
| Asymptomatic/presymptomatic transmission | Investigated; findings in full PDF |
| Transmission routes | Primary: close household contact; aerosolised virus inhalation from rodent excreta also implicated |

**Evidence Gap:**
A critical knowledge gap remains: there is no direct evidence on how long individuals infected with Andes virus remain contagious (infectious period), which limits the ability to define optimal isolation durations.

**Public Health Significance:**
Understanding transmission parameters for ANDV is especially urgent given a cruise ship outbreak in May 2026, where human-to-human transmission may have occurred in a confined setting.

## 相關疾病

Hantavirus, Andes virus (ANDV), Hantavirus cardiopulmonary syndrome (HCPS)
