## 摘要

UKHSA 發布針對安地斯病毒（Andes virus）暴露後的漢他病毒疫苗保護效力系統性文獻回顧（納入 10 項研究，證據截至 2026 年 5 月），評估疫苗對暴露於安地斯病毒的人類或動物的防護效益。

## 詳細內容

The UK Health Security Agency published a systematic evidence summary on hantavirus vaccine effectiveness following Andes virus exposure on 16 June 2026.

**Study Scope:**
- Systematic review of evidence through 19 May 2026
- **10 studies** included in the final analysis
- Examined protective vaccine effectiveness in people or animals exposed to Andes virus (ANDV)
- Document reference: GOV-21184 (PDF, 878 KB, 64 pages)

**Context — Why Andes Virus Specifically?**
Andes virus (ANDV) is the only hantavirus known to exhibit human-to-human transmission, making it a distinct public health concern compared to other hantaviruses. It causes Hantavirus Cardiopulmonary Syndrome (HCPS) with a mortality rate of 21–50%. A May 2026 cruise ship outbreak raised international concern and drove demand for evidence on available vaccines.

**Research Focus Areas:**
- Protective efficacy of existing vaccines against ANDV
- Evidence from both human and animal studies
- Assessment of vaccine candidates in the context of ANDV exposure

**Note:** Full quantitative findings, specific vaccine candidates assessed, and evidence quality conclusions are contained within the full 64-page PDF systematic evidence summary available through the GOV.UK publications portal.

## 相關疾病
