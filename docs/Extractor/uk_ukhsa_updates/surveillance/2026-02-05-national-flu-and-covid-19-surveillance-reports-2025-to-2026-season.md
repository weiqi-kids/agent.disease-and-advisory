## 摘要

National surveillance report monitoring influenza, COVID-19, and other seasonal respiratory illnesses activity for the 2025 to 2026 season, providing comprehensive epidemiological data.

## 詳細內容

This official statistics publication provides the national influenza (flu) and COVID-19 surveillance report for the 2025 to 2026 season. The report monitors:

- **COVID-19 activity**: Case numbers, hospitalizations, and trends
- **Seasonal influenza**: Strain circulation, severity, and geographic distribution
- **Other seasonal respiratory illnesses**: Including RSV and other respiratory pathogens

The surveillance system integrates multiple data sources including:
- Laboratory confirmations
- Hospital admissions
- Primary care consultations
- Mortality data
- Outbreak reports

This comprehensive monitoring enables public health officials to track disease activity, identify unusual patterns, guide clinical management, and inform vaccination strategies.

## 相關疾病

Influenza, COVID-19, Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV), and other seasonal respiratory illnesses
