## 摘要

英國健康安全署發布呼吸道合胞病毒（RSV）孕婦疫苗接種覆蓋率報告，提供疫苗接種率數據及 RSV 孕婦疫苗接種計畫的評論分析。

## 詳細內容

Insights on the vaccine uptake and commentary on the RSV (respiratory syncytial virus) vaccination programme for pregnant women. This research publication provides analysis of vaccination coverage rates and programme effectiveness in protecting newborns from RSV through maternal immunization.
