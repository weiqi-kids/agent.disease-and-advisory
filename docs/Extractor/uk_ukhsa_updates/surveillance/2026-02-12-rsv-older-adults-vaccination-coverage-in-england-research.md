## 摘要

英國健康安全署發布呼吸道合胞病毒（RSV）老年人疫苗接種覆蓋率研究報告，提供疫苗接種率數據及 RSV 老年人疫苗接種計畫的評論分析。

## 詳細內容

Insights on the vaccine uptake and commentary on the RSV (respiratory syncytial virus) vaccination programme for older adults. This research publication analyzes vaccination coverage rates and programme performance in protecting older adults from RSV-related complications.

## 相關疾病

- Respiratory syncytial virus (RSV)（呼吸道合胞病毒）
