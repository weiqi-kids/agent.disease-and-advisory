## 摘要

英國健康安全署發布呼吸道合胞病毒（RSV）老年人疫苗接種覆蓋率官方統計數據，提供 RSV 老年人疫苗接種計畫的疫苗接種率數據。

## 詳細內容

Vaccine uptake data for the respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) vaccination programme for older adults. This official statistics publication provides comprehensive vaccination coverage data for the RSV programme targeting older adults in England.
