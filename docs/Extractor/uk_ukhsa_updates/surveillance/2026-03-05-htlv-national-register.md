## 摘要

The UK HTLV National Register invites blood donors found to have HTLV and HTLV specialist clinic attenders to participate. This registry serves as an important public health surveillance tool for monitoring HTLV infection epidemiology in the United Kingdom.

## 詳細內容

Donors with HTLV and HTLV specialist clinic attenders are invited to participate in the human T-cell lymphotropic virus (HTLV) National Register. The register recruits two categories of participants: blood donors found positive during screening, and patients attending HTLV specialist clinics. Participation helps researchers better understand HTLV transmission patterns, clinical manifestations, and long-term outcomes in the UK.
