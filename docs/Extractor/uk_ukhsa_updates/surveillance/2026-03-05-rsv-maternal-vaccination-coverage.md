## 摘要

Official statistics on vaccine uptake for the RSV (respiratory syncytial virus) vaccination programme for pregnant women in England. RSV maternal vaccination aims to protect newborns through transplacental antibody transfer.

## 詳細內容

Official statistics data and reporting on vaccine uptake for the RSV (respiratory syncytial virus) vaccination programme for pregnant women. RSV is a leading cause of severe lower respiratory tract infections in infants, and maternal vaccination can protect newborns through transplacental antibody transfer. This report provides coverage data, trends over time, and regional comparisons for the RSV maternal vaccination programme in England.
