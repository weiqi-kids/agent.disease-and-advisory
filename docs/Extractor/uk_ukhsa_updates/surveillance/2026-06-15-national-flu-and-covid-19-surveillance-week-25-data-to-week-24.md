## 摘要

UKHSA 第 25 週全國流感與 COVID-19 監測報告公告，涵蓋截至 2026 年 6 月 14 日（第 24 週）的數據，監測英格蘭 COVID-19、季節性流感及其他季節性呼吸道疾病活動狀況。完整報告預定於 2026 年 6 月 18 日發布。

## 詳細內容

This is an official statistics announcement from the UK Health Security Agency for the national influenza and COVID-19 surveillance programme.

**Report Scope:**
- Monitors COVID-19 activity across England
- Tracks seasonal influenza and other respiratory illnesses
- Data covers through week 24 (up to 14 June 2026)
- Week 25 report scheduled for release 18 June 2026 at 2:00pm

**Surveillance Programme Context:**
The National Flu and COVID-19 Surveillance Report is published weekly by UKHSA, drawing on data from GP consultations, hospital admissions, ICU occupancy, laboratory testing, and mortality surveillance to track the burden of respiratory diseases in England.

**Note:** The underlying surveillance data was not yet available at the time of extraction; this record is based on the publication announcement. The full weekly report will be accessible through the GOV.UK statistics portal upon publication.

## 相關疾病

COVID-19, Influenza (seasonal flu), Respiratory syncytial virus (RSV), other seasonal respiratory illnesses
