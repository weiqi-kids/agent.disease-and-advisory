## 詳細內容

This is an official statistics announcement from the UK Health Security Agency for weekly all-cause mortality surveillance in England.

**Report Coverage:**
- Compares actual deaths in England against expected baseline numbers for each week
- Reporting period: week ending 12 July 2026
- Scheduled publication: 16 July 2026 at 2:00pm

**Purpose:**
Weekly all-cause mortality surveillance tracks whether death rates exceed or fall below anticipated levels, providing an early warning indicator for unusual public health events including infectious disease activity, extreme weather events, or other population-level health impacts.
