## 摘要

An outbreak of extensively drug-resistant (XDR) typhoid fever in Pakistan is ongoing. Extensively drug-resistant infections do not respond to most antibiotics.

## 詳細內容

An outbreak of extensively drug-resistant (XDR) typhoid fever in Pakistan is ongoing. Extensively drug-resistant infections do not respond to most antibiotics.

## 旅遊建議

**Level 1 - Practice Usual Precautions**: Travelers to Pakistan should:
- Get vaccinated against typhoid fever before travel (consult your healthcare provider about which vaccine is best)
- Practice safe food and water precautions
- Eat food that is fully cooked and served hot
- Drink only bottled, boiled, or treated water
- Avoid ice made with tap water
- Avoid raw fruits and vegetables unless you can peel them yourself
- Wash hands frequently with soap and water, especially before eating and after using the bathroom
- Seek immediate medical care if you develop prolonged fever during or after travel
- Inform your healthcare provider about your travel history and the XDR typhoid outbreak
