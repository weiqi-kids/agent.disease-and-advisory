## 摘要

Some travelers who have spent time in Mexico have been infected with multidrug-resistant (MDR) Salmonella Newport.

## 詳細內容

Some travelers who have spent time in Mexico have been infected with multidrug-resistant (MDR) <em>Salmonella</em> Newport.

## 旅遊建議

**Level 1 - Practice Usual Precautions**: Travelers to Mexico should:
- Practice safe food and water precautions
- Eat food that is fully cooked and served hot
- Avoid food from street vendors
- Drink only bottled or boiled water, or drinks made with boiled water
- Avoid ice made with tap water
- Wash hands frequently with soap and water, especially before eating
- Seek medical care if you develop diarrhea, fever, or abdominal cramps during or after travel
- Inform your healthcare provider about your travel history, as this strain is resistant to multiple antibiotics
