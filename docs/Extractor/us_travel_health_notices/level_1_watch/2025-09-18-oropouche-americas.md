## 摘要

Areas in several countries are reporting a low number of cases of Oropouche. Oropouche is spread primarily through the bites of infected midges (small flies) and mosquitoes. Country List :  Brazil, Peru, Cuba, Panama

## 旅遊建議

**Level 1 - Practice Usual Precautions**: Travelers to Brazil, Peru, Cuba, and Panama should:
- Prevent bites from midges and mosquitoes by using insect repellent
- Wear long-sleeved shirts and long pants
- Stay in places with window and door screens
- Be aware that Oropouche symptoms include sudden fever, headache, muscle aches, joint pain, and sometimes nausea and vomiting
- Seek medical care if symptoms develop during or after travel
