## 旅遊建議

**Level 1 - Practice Usual Precautions**: Travelers to Mauritania and Senegal should:
- Prevent mosquito bites by using EPA-registered insect repellents
- Wear long-sleeved shirts and long pants
- Avoid contact with livestock, particularly during birthing or slaughter
- Do not consume unpasteurized or uncooked milk or meat products
- Seek medical attention if you develop fever, headache, or muscle pain during or after travel
