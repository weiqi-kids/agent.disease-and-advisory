## 詳細內容

A case of rabies has been reported in a traveler from Morocco. Travelers potentially exposed to rabies in Morocco should immediately seek medical care. Upon returning home, travelers should follow up with their health care provider as soon as possible.

## 旅遊建議

**Level 1 - Practice Usual Precautions**: Travelers to Morocco should:
- Avoid contact with dogs, cats, bats, monkeys, and other mammals
- Seek immediate medical care if bitten or scratched by any animal
- Consider rabies pre-exposure vaccination if planning extended stays or activities that increase animal exposure risk
- Get prompt post-exposure prophylaxis if exposed to potential rabies sources
