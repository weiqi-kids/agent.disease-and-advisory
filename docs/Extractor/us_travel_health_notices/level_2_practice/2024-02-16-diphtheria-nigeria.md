## 摘要

There is an outbreak of diphtheria in several states in Nigeria. Vaccination against diphtheria is essential to protect against disease. If you are traveling to an affected area, you should be up to date with your diphtheria vaccines.

## 詳細內容

There is an outbreak of diphtheria in several states in Nigeria. Vaccination against diphtheria is essential to protect against disease. If you are traveling to an affected area, you should be up to date with your diphtheria vaccines.

## 旅遊建議

**Level 2 - Practice Enhanced Precautions**: Travelers to Nigeria should:
- Ensure diphtheria vaccination is up to date before travel
- Adults should have received a Tdap (tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis) booster within the past 10 years
- Children should be up to date with their DTaP vaccine series
- Avoid close contact with people who are sick
- Practice frequent hand washing with soap and water
- Seek immediate medical attention if you develop sore throat, fever, swollen glands, or difficulty breathing during or after travel
