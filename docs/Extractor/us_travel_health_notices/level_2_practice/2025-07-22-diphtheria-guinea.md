## 詳細內容

There are confirmed and suspect cases of diphtheria in the Kankan region of Guinea. Vaccination against diphtheria is essential to protect against disease. If you are traveling to an affected area, you should be up to date with your diphtheria vaccines.

## 旅遊建議

**Level 2 - Practice Enhanced Precautions**: Travelers to Guinea, particularly the Kankan region, should:
- Ensure diphtheria vaccination is up to date before travel
- Adults who have not received a Tdap (tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis) booster in the past 10 years should get one
- Avoid close contact with sick people
- Practice good hand hygiene
- Seek immediate medical care if you develop sore throat, fever, or swollen glands during or after travel
