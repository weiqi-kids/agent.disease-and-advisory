## 旅遊建議

**Level 2 - Practice Enhanced Precautions**: All international travelers should:
- Complete the polio vaccine series before travel if not already vaccinated
- Adults who completed the routine polio vaccine series as children and are traveling to affected areas should receive one lifetime booster dose
- Follow proper hand hygiene and food/water safety practices
- Be aware that some countries may require proof of polio vaccination for entry or exit
