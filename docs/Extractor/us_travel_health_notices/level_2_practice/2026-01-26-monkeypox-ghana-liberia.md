## 旅遊建議

**Level 2 - Practice Enhanced Precautions**: Travelers to Ghana and Liberia should practice enhanced precautions to avoid monkeypox infection. This includes:
- Avoiding contact with sick people, including those with skin or genital lesions
- Avoiding contact with dead or live wild animals
- Avoiding eating or preparing meat from wild animals (bushmeat)
- Avoiding contact with contaminated materials used by sick people or animals
- Following safe sex practices
