# Level 2 - Chikungunya in Suriname

**Alert Level:** 2 (Practice Enhanced Precautions)
**Date:** 2026-02-05
**Destination:** Suriname
**Disease:** Chikungunya

## Summary

There is an outbreak of chikungunya in Suriname. You can protect yourself by preventing mosquito bites.

## Recommendations

- Practice enhanced precautions
- Prevent mosquito bites
- Use insect repellent
- Wear long-sleeved shirts and long pants
- Stay in places with air conditioning or window/door screens
