## 摘要

截至5月10日,WHO報告在南蘇丹西赤道省Yambio的Hai-Cuba出現急性出血熱症候群病例。快速評估小組已在現場調查情況。

## 詳細內容

As of 10 May, WHO reported cases of acute haemorrhagic fever syndrome in Hai-Cuba, Yambio, Western Equatoria Region, south Sudan. A rapid assessment team is in the field to investigate the situation. Clinical samples have been collected and preliminary results are expected soon.
