## 詳細內容

10 July 2002 Disease Outbreak Reported WHO has received reports of 8 suspected cases including 5 deaths of acute haemorrhagic fever syndrome in Mbomo district, Cuvette Ouest region (see previous report) . Two new suspected cases have been reported in Mbomo since the last death on 6 June. Health authorities have identified more than 40 contacts of the suspected cases.The investigation has been hampered by the lack of community cooperation.
